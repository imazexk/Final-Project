Enhance your beauty, dont hide it

Salma Studios is located in New York City, providing on-location makeup artistry in all boroughs. Founder, Salma Tahir, is a certified MAC professional with a BA and Masters Degree in Psychology. Makeup started out as a hobby for her and turned into a passion, a passion to enhance your beauty, not hide it. Salma mainly works with MAC products and other professional cosmetic brands and also specializes in Air Brush. Salma has provided her expert consultation for weddings, proms and other private events. She has also organized and hosted fashion show events.
